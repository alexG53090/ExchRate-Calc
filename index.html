Currency Exchange Calculator

Pick your currencies to convert below

All of the exchange rate data has been pulled from the API available from fixer.io The data is updated on a daily basis, so it is not real time. Created as a mini-project on 11/9/15 while in gSchool!

This site will let you convert currencies and view currency exchange rates. You first need to enter in an amount and a currency. Then, enter in a second currency and the site will then convert your amount and first currency to the second currency. When you convert a currency, the site will also show you a table with that currencies exchange rates. The site currently only works for 32 different currencies.
